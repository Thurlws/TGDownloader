TGDOWNLOADER Step 1 of 4

Welcome to TGDownloader

TGDownloader is a personal Telegram client for managing your own music library. It connects to a Telegram bot of your choosing that you already use to receive audio files. You'll need a free Telegram API key — this only takes a few minutes.

What you're setting up
🔑 Telegram API credentials — lets TGDownloader talk to Telegram on your behalf. Free, no credit card.
📁 Stored locally only — your credentials are saved to tg_audio_config.json on this machine and never sent anywhere else.
🔒 Your account is safe — the API key only works when combined with your personal Telegram login (done separately after this).

Get Your API Credentials

Follow these steps on the Telegram developer portal. Open the link below in your browser — it's the same Telegram account you already use.

Instructions
1
Open the Telegram API portal and log in with your phone number:
⧉ my.telegram.org/auth
2
After logging in, click "API Development Tools" in the menu.
3
Fill in the form — the values don't matter much. Use anything like:
App title: TGDownloader  ·  Short name: tgdl  ·  Platform: Desktop
4
Click "Create application". You'll see your api_id (a number) and api_hash (a long hex string). Copy both and paste them below.
💡
Tip: The api_id is a short integer (e.g. 12345678). The api_hash is a 32-character hex string like a1b2c3d4…. If you see those shapes, you're in the right place.
Enter your credentials
Must be a number (digits only).
Must be a 32-character hex string.
